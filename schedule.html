Game Day Information

Home matches for the Mount St. Joseph men's volleyball team are played in the Harrington Center, located on campus at 5701 Delhi Road. Fans are encouraged to arrive early to find seating and enjoy the full game day atmosphere.

The Harrington Center provides a great environment for collegiate volleyball, and supporters play an important role in creating energy for the team during home matches.
